#### Dependencies
[ox_lib](https://github.com/overextended/ox_lib/releases) - Required to run this resource.<br/>

> [!TIP]
>
> For the best experience, you will need some custom engine sound mods - we recommend [Aquaphobic on GTA5Mods](https://www.gta5-mods.com/users/Aquaphobic/files)

This resource uses statebags to sync vehicle data between clients, allowing all players to hear your modified engine sound.
